## 3. The Monthly "Closing the Books" Checklist
*Execute on the 1st day of every month.*

- [ ] **Data Entry:** Update current prices for all holdings in Gotrade, Stockbit, and Bibit.
- [ ] **Net Worth Calc:** Total Assets - Total Liabilities = Net Worth.
- [ ] **Allocation Check:** Am I still 70% Core / 30% Satellite?
- [ ] **Cash Flow Audit:** Did I invest the full IDR 7.2M (minus expenses)?
- [ ] **Sentiment Check:** Am I feeling "Fearful" or "Greedy"? (Note this for later attribution).

---

## 4. The Annual "Strategic Review" Checklist
*Execute every January.*

### Part A: Performance Audit
- [ ] Calculate **TWR** for the year.
- [ ] Calculate **Sharpe Ratio**.
- [ ] Identify the "Best Trade" and the "Worst Trade."
- [ ] Did the "Worst Trade" violate any Module 02 Risk rules?

### Part B: IPS Update
- [ ] Has my risk tolerance changed?
- [ ] Has my monthly income increased? (Adjust the EF formula in Module 09).
- [ ] Update the **Strategic Asset Allocation (SAA)** targets for the new year.

---

## 5. Crisis Management Checklist (The "Black Swan" Protocol)
*Execute when the market drops >10% in a single week.*

- [ ] **Check IPS Limits:** Is my current Max Drawdown still within the 25% limit?
- [ ] **Review Theses:** Has the market drop changed the fundamentals of my satellite stocks?
- [ ] **Stop Panic Selling:** Am I selling because of a plan or because of fear?
- [ ] **Check Liquidity:** Is my Emergency Fund (Module 09) secure and liquid?
- [ ] **Selective Rebalancing:** If the Core is down significantly, use new salary (IDR 7.2M) to buy more VTI/IDX30 rather than the Satellites.

## 7. The "Scuttlebutt" Research Template
*For qualitative analysis of a new company.*

- [ ] **Product Test:** Have I used the product? Is it intuitive?
- [ ] **Management Check:** Watch a 30-minute interview with the CEO. Do they sound focused or promotional?
- [ ] **Competitor Check:** Read the "Risk Factors" in the competitor's annual report. What do they say about this company?
- [ ] **Glassdoor Check:** Are employees happy? (Look for mentions of "Innovation" vs "Bureaucracy").
